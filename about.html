Who We Are

OMM GANAPATI INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION & TECHNOLOGY (OMGIIT), located at NH57, Mathura Chwak, Boudh, is committed to providing quality computer education and digital skill development. Our institute offers professional computer training for students, job seekers, and working professionals through recognized courses.

Our Mission

To empower every student with modern computer knowledge, practical skills, and professional confidence so they can succeed in higher education, government examinations, and today's competitive job market.

Our Vision

To become one of Odisha's leading computer education institutes by delivering affordable, industry-oriented, and technology-driven learning experiences.
